## üé® ALL BEAUTIFUL CHARTS IN THE EXPORT (18+ Charts!)

### **EXECUTIVE VISUAL SUMMARY SECTION** (15 Charts)

#### 1. **Total Damages Breakdown (Pie Chart)**
- **Type:** Pie chart with center total
- **Shows:** Past damages vs Future damages (PV)
- **Colors:** Pink (past), Blue (future)
- **Features:** Percentage labels, exploded slices, total PV in center
- **Jury Value:** Immediate visual of how damages are split

#### 2. **Annual Economic Loss by Year (Bar Chart)**
- **Type:** Vertical bar chart
- **Shows:** Year-by-year economic losses
- **Colors:** Red (losses), Green (gains if any)
- **Features:** Clear currency formatting, gridlines
- **Jury Value:** See exactly which years had the highest losses

#### 3. **But-For vs Actual Earnings Comparison (Line Chart)**
- **Type:** Dual-line chart with filled area
- **Shows:** Green line (but-for earnings), Red line (actual earnings)
- **Features:** Shaded gray area between lines showing loss gap
- **Jury Value:** Visual representation of the earnings gap over time

#### 4. **Cumulative Damages Over Time (Area Chart)**
- **Type:** Stacked area chart
- **Shows:** How damages accumulate year after year
- **Colors:** Pink area (cumulative past), Blue area (cumulative future PV)
- **Features:** Clear boundaries between past and future accumulation
- **Jury Value:** Shows total impact grows over lifetime

#### 5. **Fringe Benefits Breakdown (Stacked Bar Chart)**
- **Type:** Stacked bar chart (or grouped for UPS)
- **Shows:** Fringe benefit components by year
- **UPS Mode:** Separate bars for H&W and Pension
- **Standard Mode:** Total fringe per year
- **Jury Value:** Demonstrates fringe benefits are substantial compensation

#### 6. **Total Compensation Package Comparison (Stacked Bar Chart)**
- **Type:** Side-by-side stacked bars
- **Shows:** Complete compensation breakdown
- **Components:** Earnings (base), Fringe (middle), Legals (top)
- **Colors:** Green shades (but-for), Red shades (actual)
- **Features:** 6-component comparison (3 but-for, 3 actual)
- **Jury Value:** Complete picture of total compensation difference

#### 7. **Economic Loss by Age Progression (Area Line Chart)**
- **Type:** Line chart with filled area
- **Shows:** How loss changes as evaluee ages
- **Features:** Age on X-axis, loss on Y-axis
- **Jury Value:** Lifecycle view of economic impact

#### 8. **Survival Probability Over Time (Line Chart)**
- **Type:** Line chart with probability scale
- **Shows:** Probability of survival at each age
- **Features:** 0-100% scale, 50% threshold line
- **Colors:** Blue line with shaded area
- **Jury Value:** Shows how mortality affects damages calculations

#### 9. **Present Value Discount Impact (Dual Line Chart)**
- **Type:** Comparison line chart with shaded difference
- **Shows:** Undiscounted future loss vs Present value
- **Colors:** Orange (undiscounted), Green (PV), Gray (discount amount)
- **Jury Value:** Demonstrates time value of money concept

#### 10. **Past vs Future Damages Distribution (Grouped Bar Chart)**
- **Type:** Side-by-side bars for each year
- **Shows:** How each year splits between past and future
- **Colors:** Pink (past), Blue (future)
- **Jury Value:** Year-by-year visualization of damage timing

#### 11. **Tax Impact on But-For Earnings (Stacked Bar Chart)**
- **Type:** Stacked bar showing tax reduction
- **Shows:** Gross earnings with tax overlay
- **Colors:** Green (after-tax), Red (tax portion)
- **Jury Value:** Shows tax adjustment is necessary and substantial

#### 12. **Legally Required Benefits Comparison (Line Chart)**
- **Type:** Dual-line comparison with shaded gap
- **Shows:** But-for vs Actual legally required benefits
- **Colors:** Green (but-for), Red (actual), Gray (difference)
- **Benefits Included:** Social Security, Medicare, unemployment insurance
- **Jury Value:** Shows even mandatory benefits are lost

#### 13. **Economic Loss as Percentage (Color-Coded Bar Chart)**
- **Type:** Bar chart with conditional coloring
- **Shows:** Annual loss as % of but-for earnings
- **Colors:** Green (<50%), Orange (50-75%), Red (>75%)
- **Features:** Threshold lines at 50% and 75%
- **Jury Value:** Shows severity of loss in relative terms

#### 14. **Prejudgment Interest Accumulation (Stacked Bar Chart)**
- **Type:** Stacked bar showing interest growth
- **Shows:** Base past damages + accumulated interest
- **Colors:** Blue (base), Orange (interest)
- **Conditional:** Only shows if interest is calculated
- **Jury Value:** Demonstrates fair compensation for delayed payment

#### 15. **UPS Fringe Benefits: H&W vs Pension (Grouped Bar Chart)**
- **Type:** Side-by-side comparison bars
- **Shows:** Health & Welfare vs Pension contributions
- **Colors:** Brown (H&W), Pink (Pension)
- **Conditional:** Only for UPS fringe method
- **Jury Value:** Detailed breakdown of UPS-specific benefits
