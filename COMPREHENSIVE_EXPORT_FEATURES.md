# Comprehensive Word Export Features

## Overview
The Word export now includes extensive visualizations and comprehensive tables designed for jury presentation and complete documentation of economic damages analysis.

## New Visualizations (Charts & Graphs)

### Executive Visual Summary Section
All charts are professionally formatted with clear labels, legends, and jury-friendly explanations.

1. **Total Damages Breakdown (Pie Chart)**
   - Shows proportion of past damages vs future damages
   - Total PV displayed in center
   - Percentages clearly labeled
   - Color-coded: Pink (past), Blue (future)

2. **Annual Economic Loss by Year (Bar Chart)**
   - Year-by-year loss visualization
   - Red bars indicate negative impact (losses)
   - Green bars for any positive scenarios
   - Clear currency formatting on Y-axis

3. **But-For vs Actual Earnings Comparison (Line Chart)**
   - Green line: But-for earnings trajectory
   - Red line: Actual/post-injury earnings
   - Shaded gray area: Economic loss gap
   - Easy visual understanding of earnings differential

4. **Cumulative Damages Over Time (Area Chart)**
   - Pink area: Cumulative past damages
   - Blue area: Cumulative future damages (PV)
   - Shows how damages accumulate year after year
   - Helps jury understand total impact over time

5. **Fringe Benefits Breakdown (Stacked Bar Chart)**
   - For UPS method: Shows H&W and Pension separately
   - For standard method: Shows total fringe
   - Year-by-year visualization
   - Clear component identification

6. **Retirement Scenarios Comparison (Grouped Bar Chart)**
   - Compares all retirement age scenarios side-by-side
   - Past damages vs Future PV for each scenario
   - Value labels on each bar
   - Easy comparison of different retirement assumptions

7. **Sensitivity Analysis Heatmap**
   - Visual representation of all discount/growth combinations
   - Green indicates higher values, red indicates lower values
   - Dollar amounts shown in each cell
   - Easy identification of range of damages

### Additional Comprehensive Tables (NEW)

#### 1. But-For Earnings Components Breakdown
**Purpose:** Show all components of but-for earnings package
**Columns:**
- Year, Age
- BF Gross Earnings
- BF After-Tax / AEF (Adjusted Earning Figure)
- H&W (if UPS method)
- Pension (if UPS method)
- Total Fringe
- BF Legally Required Benefits
- Total BF Compensation Package
- Portion (year fraction)

**Use Case:** Detailed analysis of complete but-for compensation structure

#### 2. Actual Earnings Components Breakdown
**Purpose:** Show all components of actual/post-injury earnings
**Columns:**
- Year, Age
- Actual Earnings
- Actual Fringe Benefits
- Actual Legally Required Benefits
- Total Actual Compensation Package

**Use Case:** Complete picture of actual earnings after incident

#### 3. Present Value and Survival Probability Analysis
**Purpose:** Demonstrate impact of PV discounting and mortality
**Columns:**
- Year, Age
- Annual Loss
- Survival Probability
- Future Damages (Raw)
- Future Damages (Survival-Weighted)
- PV of Future Damages

**Use Case:** Shows how survival probabilities and discounting affect damages

#### 4. Past Damages with Prejudgment Interest
**Purpose:** Detail past damages and interest accumulation
**Columns:**
- Year, Age
- Annual Loss
- Past Damages (base)
- Past Damages + Interest
- Interest Amount

**Use Case:** Shows impact of prejudgment interest on past damages

#### 5. Annual Loss Components Comparison
**Purpose:** Direct comparison showing loss calculation
**Columns:**
- Year, Age
- But-For Total Compensation
- Actual Total Compensation
- Annual Loss (difference)
- Loss Percentage

**Use Case:** Clear demonstration of year-by-year economic loss
